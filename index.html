About Nizhnvye

The Federal Empire of Nizhnyve (Nizhnyvean: Dameki C̣engarit Rešuto (IPA: [dʰaːməx t͡səŋaːɾit reʃʊtoː] Olmorian : Tǣmeki Tsongārit Āʃutō (IPA: [tɐːməqχʼiː tsɔŋaːrɐːt aeʂutoː]) , otherwise known as Nizhnyve or Acronia, is a nation in the continent of Maipela, it is a nation of around 154.622.414 people. The Capital of Nizhnyve is Lipec̣k (Nizhnyvean: Dakaroni (IPA: [daːkʱaroːŋiː]) Olmorian : Diᵹetsk (IPA: [tʷːiɣʰʷɜtsqχʼ].) Nizhnyve is a nation known for its many cultures and its diversity. Nizhnyve has many unique and great features!

All about our Culture and Langauge.

Nizhnyvean Language

Nizhnyvean is a language Isolate, meaning, no languages alive are related to it. Nizhnyvean has a few features that are unique to it, like Adjectives are an affix and that verbs are preffixes!

Our Dialects.

Nizhnyvean has many dialects, the big ones being Begasi (Pfëgahsi), Lipetsk (Lipec̣k), Dogorakan (Dògerakaè), and Dasak (Dasak).

Nizhnyvean Culture

Nizhnyvean culture is fiercly traditionalist, and we love to continue our culture, from the early days of the Israve to now, we keep our traditions alive and well!

Our Government

Nizhnyve is run be the state Dákhaf (IPA:[daːxaɸ]), This body, established back in 2320, is the main governing body of the Empire of Nizhnyve, They make decisions and then our emperor makes the final say, and approves the law. The Dakhaf has a total of 341 Members, (Increases with every census).
